1. Reflect on the differences between static and interactive visualizations.

- Static visualisation provides clear insight about specified data points, which is good for reports, infographics, and other situations where pre-defined charts are needed. 
- Interactive visualisation allowes users to explore data, customise charts output by selecting specific data points on their own. Best fit for web applications, interactive dashboards, and other situations where user interation is needed.
